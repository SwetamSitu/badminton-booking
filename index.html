Help & FAQ

How to use this site

Quick Start

  1. Open the Dashboard tab to check today's availability and WhatsApp reminder.
  2. Use New Booking to add a court manually or from a screenshot.
  3. Use Bookings to view, edit, or delete bookings.

How to vote

  1. Select your name from the poll dropdown.
  2. Choose Yes or No.
  3. Tap Submit Vote.

You can change your vote later by selecting your name again and submitting a new response.

How to add a booking

  1. Go to New Booking.
  2. Select booking person, place, date, time, and court.
  3. Add optional notes and tap Save Booking.

Screenshot upload

Upload a booking screenshot from the Places Leisure app. The site will try to read the date, time, court, and place, then fill the form for you.

WhatsApp reminder

If there is a booking today, the reminder card will show a ready-made WhatsApp message and a Send to WhatsApp Group button after the reminder time.

FAQ

Why can't I see the latest booking?

Tap Refresh. If it still does not update, close and reopen the page.

Can anyone edit or delete a booking?

Yes. Anyone with the link can update bookings, so please edit or delete carefully.

Why is my screenshot not detected?

Use a clear screenshot where date, time, court, and venue are visible. If OCR fails, add the booking manually.

Can I vote again?

Yes. Select your name and submit again. Your latest vote will be used.
